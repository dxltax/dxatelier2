The Last Time You Replied
— from "What Happens When I Stop?"

The short reply you gave that day
was the last I ever received.

Even after all the long messages I sent,
the pleading, the disappointment I poured out—
none of it moved you.
Not a single word made its way into your heart.

I counted every second,
hoping that just once,
you would ask me to stay.
But you didn’t.

Only silence,
dressed up as hope,
wore the mask of a response.

And that’s when I knew.
That day… was the last.
